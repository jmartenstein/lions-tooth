# lions-tooth

A Rust-based scifi strategy game

# The Story

The Perseus Arm remains a jagged scar of silence, a vast stretch of space where the celestial mechanics of a dozen civilizations were ground to a halt by the Great Catastrophe. For generations, the survivors drifted in the cold shadows of dead stars, clinging to the wreckage of a reality they no longer possessed the equations to solve. The Lion’s Tooth program was the first pulse of a collective heart, a desperate scattering of intent into the void. These were not ships in the traditional sense, but vessels of pure potential, cast out like spores upon the solar winds to find purchase in the ruins of the old world.

The descent onto a once-inhabited planet is a ritual of kinetic energy and heat, a violent re-entry into the cycle of time. The technology of the Lion’s Tooth does not seek to conquer the environment, but to integrate with it, burrowing into the wounded crust to find the latent heat of a world that has forgotten the sun. It is a slow, molecular awakening—a process of turning the grit of disaster into the foundations of a new architecture. The goal is not merely survival, but the patient restoration of the planetary spirit, weaving the scattered threads of a broken history into a tapestry capable of supporting life once more.

Across the darkness, these beacons began to flicker, tiny pinpricks of light signaling a reversal of the long decay. The workers of the program watch the data streams not for immediate triumph, but for the subtle shifts in equilibrium that herald a changing tide. It is a gamble played across centuries, where the success of a single seed justifies the loss of a thousand. As the first whispers of a renewed atmosphere begin to stir the dust of the Perseus Arm, the survivors realize they are no longer just ghosts of the past, but the architects of a second, more resilient Galactic Age.

# Starting Objectives

## Phase I: The Deep Rooting
  
Awaken the Sleeping Heart: Drive the vessel’s spinal column into the planetary marrow to draw forth the world’s ancient, hidden warmth.

Break the Silent Shroud: Use the ship's kinetic resonance to shatter the crystalline storms that have choked the surface since the Catastrophe.

Invoke the Old Maps: Recite the star-paths of the Lion’s Tooth to align your position with the ghosts of the former empire.

## Phase II: The Alchemical Change

Transmute the Poisoned Soil: Begin the slow, rhythmic conversion of dead salt and ash into a loam capable of holding life.

Breathe Life into the Wind: Release the "Vapors of Genesis" from the ship’s Great Vats to turn the freezing sky into a shroud of protection.

Sanctify the Landing Cairn: Transform the impact crater into a permanent sanctuary, a stone-and-steel cathedral for those who will follow.
